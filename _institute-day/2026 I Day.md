<p>Millennia Institute will be celebrating its 23<sup>rd</sup> Institute Day
on 15 May 2026, with our distinguished alumnus and Institute Advisory Committee
Member, Mr Ng Bin Hong, as Guest of Honour.</p>
<p>This year’s theme. <strong>#DoItMIWay: Lead with Purpose, Lift as One</strong> is
a clarion call for Millennians to be purpose-driven, empathetic and collaborative
as we work towards transformational goals. It reminds us that the future
we seek to build begins with the choices we make today - purposeful in
action, generous in spirit, and united in lifting one another higher. The
celebration will commemorate our staff and student achievements, both academic
and non-academic, featuring inspiring student performances that embody
the MI spirit.</p>
